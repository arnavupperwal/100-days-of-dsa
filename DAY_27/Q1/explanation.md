# Day 27 - Question 1 Solution

## 🎯 Problem
Find Intersection Point of Two Linked Lists

## 💡 Approach
Compare elements of the two arrays; when a common value is found, verify that the remaining parts of both arrays are identical – that is the intersection point.

## ⏱️ Time Complexity
O(n*m)

## 💾 Space Complexity
O(1)
