# Day 33 - Question 1 Solution

## 🎯 Problem
Infix to Postfix Conversion

## 💡 Approach
Scan the infix string, push operands to output, use a stack for operators respecting precedence, pop when higher or equal precedence operators are on stack, finally pop remaining operators.

## ⏱️ Time Complexity
O(n)

## 💾 Space Complexity
O(n)
