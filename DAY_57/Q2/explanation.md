# Day 57 - Question 2 Solution

## 🎯 Problem
Flatten Binary Tree to Linked List

## 💡 Approach
Iteratively splice left subtree to the right by finding the rightmost node of the left subtree and linking the original right subtree, then move to the next right node.

## ⏱️ Time Complexity
O(n)

## 💾 Space Complexity
O(1)
